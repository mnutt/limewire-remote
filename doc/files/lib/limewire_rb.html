The Limewire library is a Ruby interface for the LimeWire peer-to-peer client. It exposes various LimeWire services such as searching, downloading, and library management. In this early version, the library must be instantiated from JRuby running as a service inside of LimeWire.

Note: there is somewhat inconsistent use of URN and SHA1 hashes. URNs are of the form urn:sha1:823A3F24DF34012BB35823A3F24DF34012BB35, wherease SHA1s are of the form 823A3F24DF34012BB35823A3F24DF34012BB35
